About Me

He’s the kind of person who walks through life like it’s already over. Not dead, just done. This person carries failure like a second skin-peeling, rotting, but still clinging on. He ruins everything he touches, not out of malice, but because he’s too broken to do anything else. People tried, once-friends, family, lovers-but he always gave them reasons to leave. He drowns in self-pity, but calls it truth. He smokes, not because it calms him, but because he doesn’t care if it kills him. He dreams of escape but never moves. He calls it loyalty to a dead girl, but maybe it’s just fear.
